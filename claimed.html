Bamfu

iOS, Android

http://bamfu.gameovenstudios.com

Production manager

Bam fu is a game on one device where two-, three- or four people experience their HANDS CLASH IN CHAOS. Everyone is fighting over the same pebbles and physical contact is totally part of this game. In Bam fu, each player fights for her own color. Pebbles loop through the colors of the players when being tapped. In order to win, one must be quick and not afraid of blocking arms, pushing hands or squeezing fingers. A player wins the round when she holds the majority of the buttons and has secured her victory when she has won 5 rounds.
